Russian state hackers conduct a global campaign targeting Signal and WhatsApp accounts of government officials and military personnel using social engineering to gain access to verification codes and linked devices. The attacks focus on individual accounts without breaching the messaging platforms themselves. Users are warned not to share verification codes or scan unknown QR codes to prevent account compromise.
