Russia Apple VK削除で政治検閲批判

- **概要**
Russia authorities criticized Apple for removing popular VK apps from its App Store labeling the action as political censorship and questioning the company's trustworthiness The removal affected VKontakte VK Music VK Messenger VK Video Odnoklassniki and Mailru services causing disruption to social media messaging email video and educational functions The decision followed sanctions compliance claims by Apple without specifying details and led to backlash from Russian officials including Kremlin and Foreign Ministry representatives who condemned the move as politically motivated and harmful to Russian technology expansion.
