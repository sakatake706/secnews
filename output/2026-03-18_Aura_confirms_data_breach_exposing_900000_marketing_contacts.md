- **タイトル**
Aura confirms data breach exposing 900000 marketing contacts

- **概要**
Identity protection company Aura suffered a data breach due to a voice phishing attack targeting an employee which exposed nearly 35000 customer records and 900000 marketing contacts. The incident involved unauthorized access to names and email addresses but did not compromise Social Security Numbers or financial information. Aura is conducting an internal review with cybersecurity experts and has informed law enforcement authorities. Notifications to affected individuals will be sent soon.
