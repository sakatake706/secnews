ロシアボットネット運営者有罪判決

- **概要**
Russian national Ilya Angelov sentenced to 24 months in US prison for managing a botnet used in ransomware attacks targeting American companies The botnet operated by the group known as Mario Kart distributed malware and spam on a large scale and sold access to compromised machines to other criminals The campaign reached up to 700000 phishing emails daily and compromised around 3000 machines per day Angelov monetized the botnet by selling access to bots used in ransomware extortion schemes One ransomware variant involved was BitPaymer which caused significant financial damage The operation later partnered with a group linked to IcedID malware Angelov used aliases milan and okart during his activities。
