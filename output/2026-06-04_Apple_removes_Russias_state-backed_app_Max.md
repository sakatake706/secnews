- **タイトル**
Apple removes Russias state-backed app Max

- **概要**
Apple removed Russia's state-backed messaging app Max from its App Store following compliance with sanctions regulations. The app became unavailable for download on iPhones and iPads while existing users can still use it without updates or notifications. Russian officials criticized the move as unfriendly and noted the impact on about 20 million users. Max remains available on Android platforms and is promoted domestically as an alternative to foreign messaging services. The removal reflects ongoing tensions and control over digital communications in Russia.

- **備考**
Apple's removal of Max aims to enforce sanctions and limit Russia's digital influence. The app's integration with government services raises concerns about surveillance. The decision may reduce Max's user engagement and push users towards other platforms. Similar removals of VPN apps indicate increased regulatory pressure.
