- **タイトル**
Russian Office flaw攻撃

- **概要**
Russian state hackers linked to military intelligence exploited a newly patched Microsoft Office vulnerability tracked as CVE-2026-21509 to target government bodies in Ukraine and European countries. Attackers used malicious documents disguised as official correspondence to deploy Covenant malware and variants MiniDoor and PixyNetLoader. The campaign attributed to APT28 group intensified amid ongoing conflict. Microsoft released a patch early January. Attacks expected to increase if updates delayed.
